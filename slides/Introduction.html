Q: How do thermodynamic quantities react to a (small) change in dynamics?

  1. Take a big, messy system described microscopically by \(\mathcal{L}_0\)
  2. Change the dynamics \(\mathcal{L}_0\longrightarrow \mathcal{L}_0+\Delta \mathcal{L}\) (EFT corrections)
  3. How do quantities like Entropy (\(S\)) or Energy (\(M\)) change?

Compare states in original and perturbed theories

Will show: \(\Delta M\propto -\Delta S\) very generally in certain regimes
