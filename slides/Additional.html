The thermodynamic potential

\[ G_{0}(\mu,T)=\frac{(1-\mu^{2})^{2}}{16\pi T}\ , \quad \Delta G(\mu,T)=-\frac{4\pi \epsilon}{5}\frac{T\mu^{4}}{(1-\mu^{2})} \]

Compute \(\Delta M_{\rm ext}\) using \(\mu(Q,T)\approx 1-2\pi Q T\) at small T

\[ \Delta M_{\rm ext}=\lim_{T\to 0}\Delta G\left(\mu(Q,T),T\right)=-\frac{\epsilon}{5Q} \]

Explicit Example: RN Black Holes

Compute \(T_0\), \(\Delta S\) at \(M=Q\times (1+\delta)\)

Non-Analyticity and the \(T\longrightarrow 0\) Limit

The zero-\(T\) limit generically contains non-analyticities, as seen from

\[ \lim_{T\to 0}\left(\frac{\partial M}{\partial \epsilon}\right)_{T,\Q}=\lim_{T\to 0}-T\left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial \epsilon}\right)_{M,\Q} \]

\(\left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial \epsilon}\right)_{M,\Q}\) must diverge as \(T^{-1}\) at low-\(T\), though \(S(M,\Q,\epsilon)\) needn't

Related: when \(\Delta T(M,\Q)\) is comparable to \(T_0(M,\Q)\), \(S\) may not be analytic in \(\epsilon\).

\(\Delta M\approx -T_0\Delta S\) derived for \(\Delta T\ll T_0\) where analytic \(S\approx S_0+\epsilon\Delta S\)

Non-Analyticity and the \(T\longrightarrow 0\) Limit

Non-analyticity in \(\epsilon\) is physically sensible.

Expect: unperturbed extremal state continues to exist only for one sign of \(\epsilon\)

Some typical forms of low-\(T\) corrections: [Cano et al., 1910.14324]:

\[ S(M,\Q,\epsilon)\approx S^{0}_{\rm ext.}(\Q)+F(M,\Q)\sqrt{M-M_{\rm ext}^{0}(\Q)+\epsilon} \]

The unperturbed extremal state \(M=M_{\rm ext}^{0}(\Q)\) only has real entropy for \(\epsilon>0\)

Whether \(S\) has analytic expansion in \(\epsilon\) depends on \(\frac{M-M_{\rm ext}^{0}}{M}\), or equivalently on \(\frac{T-T_{0}}{T}\)

Non-Analyticity and the \(T\longrightarrow 0\) Limit

Some typical forms of low-\(T\) corrections: [Cano et al., 1910.14324]:

\[ S(M,\Q,\epsilon)\approx S^{0}_{\rm ext.}(\Q)+F(M,\Q)\sqrt{M-M_{\rm ext}^{0}(\Q)+\epsilon} \]

For \(\Delta M\approx -T_0\Delta S\), work at \(M=(1+\delta)\times M_{\rm ext}^{0}\) with \(0<\delta \ll 1\) while maintaining \(\Delta T\ll T_0(M,\Q)\), which are opposing constraints

\[ S(M,\Q,\epsilon)\approx S^{0}_{\rm ext.}(\Q)+F(M,\Q)\sqrt{M_{\rm ext}^{0}(\Q)\times \delta+\epsilon} \]

\(\Delta M\approx -T_0\Delta S\) holds for \(|\epsilon|\gg M_{\rm ext}\delta\)

Changed to \(\Delta M\approx -\frac{1}{2}T_0\Delta S\) holds for \(|\epsilon|\ll M_{\rm ext}\delta\)

Both sides computed at leading order in \(\delta,\epsilon\)

\(\Delta S>0 \implies ?\)

If \(\Delta S>0\), then \(\partial_{\epsilon}M_{\rm ext}=-T\partial_{\epsilon}S\) suggests \(\Delta M_{\rm ext}>0\)

This reasoning first used in [Cheung, Remmen, Liu, 1801.08546] as evidence for WGC
